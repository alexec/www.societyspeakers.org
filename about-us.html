The Society Speakers Programme

Specially designed to take us from the club meeting room where we practice, out into live speaking situations. These new experiences give us more confidence and further develop our speaking skills. Usually these speeches will be for a charity or a cause so we can also deliver a benefit to society.

You will receive support and mentoring to…

  • Research and prepare compelling speeches
  • Practice and get feedback to sharpen your message
  • Find interesting and relevant places to speak

Society Speakers is a Toastmasters Club run by our members on a not for profit basis. This keeps the cost of membership very low and accessible to all.

Toastmasters International provide the educational programme with excellent manuals and support materials: www.toastmasters.org
