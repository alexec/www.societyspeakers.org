Society Speakers London Toastmasters Club

Practise, feed back, and deliver your message

Do you want to become a confident public speaker? Do you want to do this in a supportive environment surrounded by friendly people who want to help you? Then Society Speakers is for you!

Society Speakers is a fun and exciting public speaking club that meets in Central London every month. You can learn and pracise public speaking in a friendly and supportive enironment.

The best way to find out more is to come to a meeting. As a guest, it's free, you can see what we do, and meet members who can tell you more.

We meet in Central London from 7pm to 9pm on the first and third Wednesday of every month. Our venue is the Fitzrovia Community Centre, W1W 6DL.
