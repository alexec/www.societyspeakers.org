Society Speakers

For those who want to become amazing public speakers and help others.

Who are Society Speakers?

Do you want to become and amazing, confident public speaker? Do you want to do in a safe environment surrounded by friendly people who want to help you? Do you want to use the skill you've learned to help other, to promote charities? Then Society Speakers is for you.

We are a Toastmasters club to help people both grow their public skills, and to look to take those skills to other places to help charities and organisations they work with.

How can I get involved?

Come along to our next meeting and find out more! We meet the first and third Wednesday of every month in the Fitzrovia Community Centre.
